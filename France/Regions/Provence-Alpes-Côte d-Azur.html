Provence Alpes Côte d'Azur

Overview

Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur (PACA), located in southeastern France, is famous for its sunny Mediterranean coastline, lavender fields, historic towns, and Alpine villages. Major cities include Marseille, Nice, and Avignon.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Marseille
  • Regional Language: French (Provençal Occitan also spoken)
  • Population: ~5 million
  • Famous For: French Riviera, lavender, Roman ruins, art heritage
  • Fun Fact: The Cannes Film Festival is held here every year!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Provence-Alpes-Côte d’Azur is from May to October, when the weather is warm and ideal for beach trips, lavender blooming, and festivals.
