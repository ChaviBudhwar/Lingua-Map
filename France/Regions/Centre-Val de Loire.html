Centre-Val de Loire

Overview

Centre-Val de Loire, located in the heart of France, is known as the “Garden of France” due to its lush landscapes, vineyards, and vast châteaux. The region is steeped in Renaissance history, with the Loire Valley recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Orléans
  • Regional Language: French
  • Population: ~2.5 million
  • Famous For: Loire castles, wine, Joan of Arc, gardens
  • Fun Fact: The Château de Chambord has 365 fireplaces — one for each day of the year!

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit is from May to October when the gardens are in full bloom and château tours are most enjoyable. Spring and autumn offer mild weather and fewer crowds.
