Spain

Country Overview

Spain is a southwestern European country known for its rich history, vibrant culture, art, and cuisine. It is located on the Iberian Peninsula. From the historic cities of Madrid and Barcelona to the sun-kissed coastlines, Spain offers a mix of old-world charm and modern flair.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Madrid
  • Official Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~47 million
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Time Zone: Central European Time (CET)
  • Neighboring Countries: France, Andorra, Portugal
  • Fun Fact: Spain is home to the world’s oldest restaurant, Sobrino de Botín, founded in 1725!

  • Emblem:
  • National Anthem: "Marcha Real" (the Royal March)
  • National Animal: Bull (symbol of Spain’s cultural traditions)

Language & Linguistics

The official language of Spain is Spanish (also known as Castilian). Spanish is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world, with over 460 million speakers. In Spain, there are also regional languages like Catalan, Basque, and Galician, which have official status in their respective regions.

Culture

  • Art: Pablo Picasso, Salvador Dalí, Francisco Goya
  • Architecture: Sagrada Familia, Alhambra, Park Güel
  • Music: Flamenco, Classical guitar
  • Dance: Flamenco and traditional regional dances
  • Festivals: Running of the Bulls, La Tomatina, Feria de Abril
  • Literature: Don Quixote by Miguel de Cervantes
