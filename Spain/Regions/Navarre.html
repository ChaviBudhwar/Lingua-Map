Navarre

Overview

Navarre is a diverse autonomous community in northern Spain, blending lush forests, mountainous landscapes, and rich Basque cultural heritage. Known for its historical significance, it offers a mix of nature, tradition, and modern life.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Pamplona
  • Regional Languages: Spanish, Basque
  • Population: ~650,000
  • Famous For: Running of the Bulls, Basque culture, natural parks
  • Fun Fact: Navarre was once a powerful medieval kingdom with strong ties to both Spain and France!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Navarre is during spring (April–June) and autumn (September–October), ideal for festivals and enjoying the natural parks.
