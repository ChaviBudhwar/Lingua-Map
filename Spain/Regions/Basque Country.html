Basque Country

Overview

The Basque Country is located in the north of Spain, near the Bay of Biscay. It's a region with a unique culture and its own language, Basque (Euskera). It is known for its gastronomy, green landscapes, and modern cities such as Bilbao and San Sebastián.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Vitoria-Gasteiz
  • Regional Languages: Spanish, Basque (Euskera)
  • Population: ~2.2 million
  • Famous for: Gastronomy, culture, and mountains
  • Fun Fact: Basque is one of the oldest languages in Europe!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit the Basque Country is in spring and autumn, when the weather is pleasant and there are fewer tourists.
