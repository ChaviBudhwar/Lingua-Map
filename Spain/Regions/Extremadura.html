Extremadura

Overview

Extremadura is in the west of Spain, near Portugal. It's a peaceful region with many natural parks, ancient towns, and Roman monuments. It has a rich history and many rural traditions.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Mérida
  • Regional Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~1.05 million
  • Famous for: Roman ruins, Iberian ham, nature
  • Fun Fact: Mérida has one of the best-preserved Roman theaters in the world!

Best Time to Visit

It’s ideal to visit Extremadura in spring (April–June) or autumn (September–November) when the weather is mild and there are lots of flowers or fall colors.
