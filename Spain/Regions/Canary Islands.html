Canary Islands

Overview

The Canary Islands are located in the Atlantic Ocean, near the coast of Africa. They are known for their warm year-round climate, beautiful beaches, and impressive volcanoes. It's a popular tourist destination for those seeking sun and nature.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Las Palmas de Gran Canaria and Santa Cruz de Tenerife
  • Regional Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~2.2 million
  • Famous for: Beaches, volcanoes, and nature
  • Fun Fact: Mount Teide, in Tenerife, is the highest peak in Spain!

The best time to visit the Canary Islands is all year round, thanks to their mild and warm climate.
