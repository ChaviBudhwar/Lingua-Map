Balearic Islands

Overview

The Balearic Islands are located in the Mediterranean Sea. They are famous for their beaches, warm climate, and culture. The main islands are Mallorca, Menorca, Ibiza, and Formentera.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Palma de Mallorca
  • Regional Languages: Spanish, Catalan (Balearic)
  • Population: ~1.2 million
  • Famous for: Beaches, parties, nature
  • Fun Fact: Ibiza is known worldwide for its nightlife!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit the Balearic Islands is in spring and summer (April to September), to enjoy the sun and the beaches.
