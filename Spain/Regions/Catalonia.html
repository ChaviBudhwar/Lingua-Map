Catalonia

Overview

Catalonia is in the northeast of Spain. It’s known for its unique culture, the Catalan language, and the city of Barcelona. It has beautiful beaches, mountains, and many museums and famous architecture.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Barcelona
  • Regional Languages: Catalan, Spanish
  • Population: ~7.7 million
  • Famous for: Gaudí, football, beaches, Mediterranean food
  • Fun Fact: The Sagrada Familia temple is still not finished!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Catalonia is in spring (April–June) or autumn (September–October) for good weather and fewer tourists.
