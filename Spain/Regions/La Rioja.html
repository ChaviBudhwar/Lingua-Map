La Rioja

Overview

La Rioja is located in northern Spain and is famous for its high-quality wine. The region has a varied landscape with vineyards, mountains, and rivers. The wine culture is very important to its people.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Logroño
  • Regional Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~320,000
  • Famous for: Wine, wineries, natural landscape
  • Fun Fact: La Rioja is one of the most famous wine regions in the world!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit La Rioja is in autumn (September–November), when it is the grape harvest season and new wine.
