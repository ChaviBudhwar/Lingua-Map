Madrid

Overview

Madrid is a region in central Spain known for its museums, parks, and nightlife. The city of Madrid is the capital and has an important cultural and historical heritage.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Madrid
  • Regional Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~6.7 million (metropolitan area)
  • Famous for: Museums, parks, nightlife
  • Fun Fact: Madrid has one of the largest art museums in the world, the Prado Museum!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Madrid is in spring (March–May) and autumn (September–November) because of its pleasant weather.
