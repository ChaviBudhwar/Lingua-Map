Castile-La Mancha

Overview

Castile-La Mancha is located in the center of Spain. It is famous for its windmills, wine, and the novel "Don Quixote of La Mancha". The region features vast plains and many historic towns.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Toledo
  • Regional Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~2 million
  • Famous for: Windmills, wine, Don Quixote
  • Fun Fact: It has more vineyards than any other region in the world.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Castile-La Mancha is in the spring (April–June) and autumn (September–October) due to the pleasant weather.
