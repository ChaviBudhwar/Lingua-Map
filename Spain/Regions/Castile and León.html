Castile and León

Overview

Castile and León is a large region in the northwest of Spain, known for its history, architecture, and nature. It has many ancient cities, castles, and cathedrals. The region is important in the history of Spain.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Valladolid
  • Regional Language: Spanish
  • Population: ~2.4 million
  • Famous for: Castles, historic cities, and nature
  • Fun Fact: Castile and León has the highest number of castles in Spain.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Castile and León is during spring and autumn, when the weather is pleasant and there are fewer tourists.
