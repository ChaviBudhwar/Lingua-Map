Lingua Map
A Journey Through Languages & Lands

Introduction

Lingua Map is an interactive website that explores three countries: Italy, France, and Spain. I created it by combining the information I learned in class with my personal interests. The site brings together key facts about each country and its regions — including maps, cultural highlights, and language insights. It’s designed to be simple, visual, and engaging way to explore and connect what I’ve studied.

Objectives

  • Explore the cultures and languages of the countries I've studied.
  • Use HTML and CSS to create an interactive and engaging experience.
  • Apply research skills to gather and organize content creatively.
  • Improve my ability to communicate information in multiple languages.
