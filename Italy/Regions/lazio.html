Lazio

Overview

Lazio is a central region of Italy, famous for its capital, Rome, the heart of the Roman civilization and home to the Vatican City. The region offers a unique mix of history, art, nature, and food.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Rome
  • Language: Italian
  • Population: Around 5.7 million
  • Famous for: Ancient Rome, the Vatican, traditional cuisine
  • Fun Fact: Rome is the only city in the world that hosts an entire country inside it!

When to Visit

Spring and autumn are the best seasons to visit Lazio: the weather is pleasant and cities are less crowded. Summer is lively but hot, while winter is ideal for quiet exploration.
