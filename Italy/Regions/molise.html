Overview

Molise is one of Italyâ€™s smallest regions, located in the south-central part of the country. Known for its unspoiled nature, medieval villages, and traditional culture, it offers an authentic experience far from mass tourism.

When to Visit

Spring and summer are ideal to visit Molise thanks to mild weather and numerous traditional festivals. Autumn offers scenic landscapes and seasonal flavors. In winter, winter sports are possible in the mountainous areas.
